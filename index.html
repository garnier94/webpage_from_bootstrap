Remy Garnier

remy.garnier@cdiscount.com

I am a PhD student in machine learning and statistics at Analyse Geométrie Modelisation (CREST) in Cergy-Poontoise University.
My PhD Thesis is a collaboration between a company, CDiscount, based in Bordeaux, and the laboratory. My main research topics consists in the application of time series methods to the prediction of demand. More specifically, i am interested in methods for multi-time series forecasting. I'm interested in several methods to compute this prediction : hierachical bayesian modelisation, predictor agregation, Reccurent Neural Network
