Rémy Garnier

remy.garnier@ext.cdiscount.com

I am a Ph.D. student in machine learning and statistics at Analyse Geométrie Modelisation (CREST) in Cergy-Pontoise University under the supervision of Paul Doukhan and Joseph Rynkiewicz. My Ph.D. thesis is a collaboration between the laboratory AGM and a French E-commerce retailer, CDiscount, based in Bordeaux.
The goal of the thesis is to develop methods to predict the sales of different products at CDiscount. More generally, I am interested in a method to forecast multiple time series at once in presence of multiple possible sources of data. Current methods of interest include :

  • Application of classical neural networks and recurrent neural networks to high-dimensional time series prediction
  • High-dimensional auto-regressive vectorial models
  • Hierarchical reconciliation
I have also published theoretical results on time series, mostly concentration results and deviation bound for machine learning in dependant settings. My Ph.D. will be finished in December 2021. I am looking for a job in applied machine learning reasearch after this date.
